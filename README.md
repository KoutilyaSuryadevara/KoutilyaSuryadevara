## 💡 Engineering Principles

> *"Reliability is not a feature — it is an architecture decision made at every layer."*

- **Eliminate toil** before it eliminates your team's velocity
- **Instrument everything** — you can't improve what you can't measure  
- **Design for failure** — assume the network is unreliable, disks fail, and services crash
- **GitOps everything** — infrastructure drift is a security and reliability risk
- **Cost is a reliability signal** — runaway spend often indicates runaway processes
